The problem

You can write code. AI can write code. Everyone can write code now.

The bottleneck isn't building — it's knowing what to build and for whom.

Your README describes your tech stack. Your customer doesn't care about your tech stack. They care about their problem.

Market Lens makes you see your product the way your customer sees it — not the way your IDE sees it.

4 lenses, 4 hard questions

🔍
Myopia Check
"Are you selling the drill — or solving the hole?"
⚖️
Value Perception
"What does the customer get vs what they pay — and not just money?"
🎯
Satisfaction Risk
"Where will expectations break — even if the code works perfectly?"
🔄
Retention Signal
"After the first use, why would they come back tomorrow?"

How it works

1

Silent scan

Reads your README, landing pages, pricing, onboarding, error pages — extracts what your product promises.

2

3 context questions

Your target user, the problem you solve, the alternatives. With recommended answers based on what it found.

3

4 challenges

Lens by lens, it presents findings, challenges you, pushes back on vague answers. Like a sparring partner, not a reviewer.

4

PRODUCT-AUDIT.md

Findings, risk levels, and top 3 action items — saved to your project. Run again after you've acted on them.
